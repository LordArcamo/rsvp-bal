My Story

About

I'm Lord Arcamo — Head of Idea at Fazed Digital, a creative agency based in Iligan City, Philippines. I design and develop structured, SEO-driven websites that scale businesses — from local Filipino brands to US-based construction companies.

My process is design-first. Everything starts in Figma — wireframes, user flows, high-fidelity prototypes. From there I build on WordPress with Elementor, WooCommerce, and whatever stack the project needs. Clients get sites they can manage independently.

I'm tech-savvy and tool-agnostic. Whether it's Go High Level, System IO, Mailchimp, or SemRush — throw it my way and watch me master it. I've also integrated Claude AI into my workflow for faster content creation, prototyping, and problem-solving.

My approach is simple — prioritizing functionality and clarity for solutions. Whether it's an eco-cleaning platform, a construction company site, or a SaaS landing page, I bring the same craft and attention to detail.
