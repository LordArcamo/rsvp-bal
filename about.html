Our Story

About EASTLONE Cafe

A cozy coffee shop built for people who want more than a quick caffeine fix.

EASTLONE Cafe is a cozy coffee shop in Iligan City created for people who want more than a quick caffeine fix. It is a place to unwind, connect, create, and enjoy thoughtfully prepared drinks in a calm and welcoming setting.

Located in Purok Santan, Sta. Felomina, the café has quickly built a reputation for its warm atmosphere, quality coffee, and approachable service. Customers describe it as a hidden gem, a relaxing roadside café, and a comfortable place for dates, quiet time, or remote work.

At EASTLONE Cafe, every cup is meant to make people feel at home. Whether you are stopping in for your favorite latte, meeting friends, or simply taking a break from the day, the goal is simple: serve good coffee in a space that feels good to be in.
