Who We Are

More Than a Cup of Coffee

At EASTLONE Cafe, coffee is more than a drink — it's a pause, a reset, and a reason to gather. Tucked in Purok Santan, Sta. Felomina, our café is built for people who love good coffee, comfortable spaces, and unhurried moments.

  • Cozy and relaxing café atmosphere
  • Specialty coffee and handcrafted drinks
  • Great for solo coffee dates, group catch-ups, and work sessions
  • Dine-in, takeaway, delivery, and drive-through available
